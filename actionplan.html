Purpose • Growth • Discipleship

Action Plan for Ongoing Spiritual and Professional Development

Spiritual Development Goals

1. Deepen Daily Discipleship

  • Daily Scripture Study: Study for 20–30 minutes each day with a focus on doctrine and revelation.
  • Prayer Routine: Begin and end each day with intentional prayer.
  • Weekly Pondering: Reflect and journal spiritual impressions weekly.

2. Strengthen Christlike Leadership

  • Lead by Example: Practice humility and integrity.
  • Service: Participate in meaningful service monthly.
  • Mentorship: Support someone spiritually or academically.

3. Build Spiritual Resilience

  • Temple Worship: Attend monthly.
  • Purposeful Fasting: Fast with intent and record insights.
  • Spiritual Maintenance Plan: Prepare a grounding strategy for stressful times.

Professional Development Goals

1. Advance Technical Expertise

  • Targeted Learning: Complete one IT course every 2–3 months.
  • Certifications: Identify and prepare for 1–2 relevant certifications.
  • Project Application: Apply new skills in real projects.

2. Strengthen Communication & Writing

  • Weekly Writing Practice: Improve clarity and structure.
  • Feedback Loop: Request monthly writing feedback.
  • Technical Simplification: Practice explaining complex concepts simply.

3. Build a Long-Term Career Path

  • Portfolio Updates: Refresh quarterly.
  • Networking: Connect with one new professional monthly.
  • 12-Month Goal: Define a target IT role and steps to reach it.
