🔌 Three‑Phase Power Distribution & Motor Control Systems

Industrial electrical engineering portfolio showcasing 3‑phase (400V) power distribution, Variable Speed Drive (VSD) integration, motor control circuits, and safe isolation (LOTO) protocols compliant with BS 7671 standards.

Industrial Electrical Engineering Overview

This section documents hands‑on installation, testing, and maintenance of low‑voltage and 3‑phase (400V) infrastructure, aligned with UK regulatory frameworks and modern factory automation requirements.

🛠️ Core Technical Competencies Demonstrated

1. Three‑Phase (400V) Power Distribution

  • Installation Mastery: Laying out industrial distribution networks, mounting three‑phase distribution boards (DBs), terminating SWA cabling, and wiring multi‑pole circuit breakers.
  • Testing & Commissioning: Performing phase rotation tests, insulation resistance checks, and earth fault loop impedance (Zs) verification using industrial multi‑function testers.

2. Variable Speed Drives (VSD / VFD) & Automation

  • Control Signal Wiring: Terminating 3‑phase incoming power, motor outputs, and low‑voltage control loops (0‑10V / 4‑20mA instrumentation loops) for automated machine speed control.
  • Parameter Calibration: Programming acceleration/deceleration ramps, motor frequency thresholds, and electronic overload limits via drive HMIs.

3. Industrial Motor Control & Submersible Pumps

  • Control Panel Design: Wiring and fault‑finding on Star‑Delta starters, DOL starters, float switches, and heavy‑duty contactors.
  • System Reliability: Implementing preventive maintenance (PPM) routines on industrial pump motors to eliminate unexpected downtime.

4. Safe Isolation & Compliance

  • LOTO Protocol: Strict adherence to safe isolation procedures using approved voltage indicators (AVIs) and Lock‑Out/Tag‑Out padlocks before intrusive electrical work.
