My Guiding Principles

1. Seek the Lord’s Will with a Single Eye

Inspired by 3 Nephi 13:19–22, this principle reminds me to align my priorities with the Lord’s will. It guides my decisions academically, spiritually, and professionally.

2. Build Peace Through Christlike Influence

I want to bring calm, clarity, and fairness into every environment I’m part of. This principle shapes how I communicate and how I treat others.

3. Honour Sacred Covenants and Temple Blessings

Remembering my covenants helps me choose integrity and stay grounded in eternal purpose.

Materials I Am Gathering

  • Talks: Elder Bednar, “Gather Together in One All Things in Christ”
  • Temple reflections and personal experiences
