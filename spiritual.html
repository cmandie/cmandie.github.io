Guiding Principles

1. Faith in Jesus Christ as a Daily Guide

Faith is a daily practice that shapes my decisions, learning, and character. This principle reminds me to seek the Savior’s guidance in both spiritual and professional challenges. When I rely on Him, I gain clarity, peace, and direction. Faith helps me approach my studies with purpose and my work with humility, knowing the Lord magnifies my efforts when I trust Him.

2. Integrity and Accountability

Integrity means doing what is right even when no one is watching. In discipleship and professional life, accountability builds trust and spiritual strength. This principle guides how I communicate, complete assignments, and interact with others. It reminds me that honesty is both a spiritual law and a professional expectation.

3. Service and Christlike Love

Christlike service transforms ordinary actions into meaningful discipleship. This principle teaches me to lead with compassion, patience, and genuine concern for others. Whether helping a classmate or supporting someone with a technical issue, service allows me to reflect the Savior’s example.
